Social and Cultural ActivitiesClosely associated with the successful management and functioning of Gaushala; played an active role in Gau-Seva Sangh Sanstha, Sagar, Madhya Pradesh for the cause of cow protection; engage youth in the constructive work for removing the barriers of caste and class;and making efforts for the development of orphanages and Deaf and Dumb schools and old age homes
Special InterestsHelp the people in distress and raising their economic and social status;and providing medical and other socio-economic facilities to the lowest of the lowest strata of society and instil a sense of self respect in them.
Sports and ClubsHockey, kabaddi, chess and cricket; won a number of prizes in 100 mts. and 200 mts. race events at school level
Favourite Pastimes and RecreationSinging and playing Mouth-organ
Countries VisitedAustralia, China, Mangolia and Thailand
Other InformationActively participated in the Total Revolution movement started by Lok Nayak Jayaprakash Narayan in 1975; went to Sagar and Jabalpur jails for 16 months under Maintenance of Internal Security Act (MISA) in protest against imposition of Emergency; started movement for solving the problems faced by students and opened library for their assistance; joined politics in 1982 and since then actively associated with the National, State, local movements and programmes initiated by B.J.P.
